What is reuse?

Reuse is a nonprofit platform that helps people give away everyday items—like clothes, furniture, books, and electronics—to charity. It serves as a one-stop donation point, collecting goods on behalf of trusted partner charities.

By making donating used items more accessible, it reduces waste, supports environmental sustainability, and ensures donated items reach the communities that need them most.
