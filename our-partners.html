Oxfam

Oxfam Ireland is a nonprofit organization that works to fight global poverty and injustice. They run development programs, respond to humanitarian crises, and advocate for policy change.

Enable Ireland

Enable Ireland provides therapeutic, clinical and early intervention services for children and adults with disabilities and their families across Ireland.

Charity Regulatory Authority number: 20006617

Irish Cancer Society

Supports people affected by cancer through research funding, patient information and support services, advocacy, and public awareness campaigns.

Charity Regulatory Authority number: 20009502

SVP

Society of St. Vincent de Paul (SVP) is Ireland's largest voluntary charitable organization, providing practical assistance to individuals and families experiencing poverty.

Charity Regulatory Authority number: 20013806
